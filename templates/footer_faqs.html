Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find answers to common questions about Health Stack System.

1. Account Related

Q1: How do I create an account?
You can register using your email and phone number on the signup page.

Q2: Can I update my profile details?
Yes, you can update your information anytime from your account settings.

2. Appointments

Q3: How can I book an appointment?
Search for doctors and select a time slot to book your appointment.

Q4: Can I cancel or reschedule appointments?
Yes, you can manage your appointments from your dashboard.

3. Doctors & Services

Q5: How do I find doctors?
You can search by specialization, location, or doctor name.

Q6: Can I communicate with doctors?
Yes, Health Stack allows patients to interact with doctors.

4. Payments

Q7: What payment methods are available?
You can pay using UPI, debit/credit cards, or online payment gateways.

Q8: Are payments secure?
Yes, we use secure payment systems to protect your transactions.

5. Medical Records

Q9: Can I access my medical records?
Yes, your health records are stored securely and accessible anytime.

Q10: Can I share my reports with doctors?
Yes, you can share your reports through the platform.

6. Security & Privacy

Q11: Is my data secure?
Yes, we use encryption and security measures to protect your data.

Q12: Who can access my information?
Only authorized users and healthcare providers can access your data.
